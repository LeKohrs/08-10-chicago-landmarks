Chicago Theatre

This French Baroque-style “Wonder Theatre of the World" hosts a variety of entertainment in the Loop area.

Navy Pier

A 50-acre playground of entertainment, museums, activities, restaurants, and shops on the beautiful shoreline of Lake Michigan.

Crown Fountain

An interactive work of public art and video sculpture featured in Millennium Park featuring glass-brick towers, LED displays, and water.

Buckingham Fountain

See breathtaking light and water shows right in the center of Grant Park at one of the largest fountains in the world.

Wrigley Field

Featuring ivy-covered walls and a manual scoreboard, this charming and iconic baseball field is home of the legendary Chicago Cubs.

Millennium Park

A “new kind of town square” in the heart of the city featuring collections of architecture, public art, landscape design and events for all.

Cloud Gate

AKA “The Bean” — Measuring 66 feet long and standing more than 30 feet high, Cloud Gate is one of the largest sculptures of its kind in the world.

Field Museum of Natural History

More than 20 million artifacts in interactive and imaginative displays await in one of the largest natural history museums in the world.

Lincoln Park Zoo

Have a wildlife experience with animals from around the world for free in this 35-acre zoo that is one of the oldest in the nation.

Skydeck

Offering interactive exhibits and spectacular views, the Skydeck Ledge is a glass balcony extending four feet outside the 103rd floor of Willis Tower.
